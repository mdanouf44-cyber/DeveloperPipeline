## 🏆 2. Winning Content Archetypes

LinkedIn posts on the `Mohammad Anouf Saani (www.linkedin.com/in/mohammad-anouf-saani)` account perform best when structured around the following five archetypes (ranked in order of priority and performance):

### Archetype 1: Founder Psychology Contrarian (Plain Text)
* **Performance:** Top performer (averaging 400+ impressions per post). Generates the strongest emotional response and highest comment engagement.
* **The Recipe:** Challenge a widely-held belief $\rightarrow$ validate the reader's quiet frustration $\rightarrow$ offer a sharp reframe $\rightarrow$ ask a pointed question.
* **Key Emotion:** Validation and relief ("Finally, someone said it").

### Archetype 2: Loaded Poll (Interactive Text)
* **Performance:** Highest-impression format (averaging 500+ impressions). Driven by the LinkedIn algorithm's reward for interaction.
* **The Recipe:** State a relatable tension or dilemma $\rightarrow$ provide 4 emotionally-loaded options where there is no obvious "right" answer $\rightarrow$ prompt voters to explain their choice in comments.
* **Key Emotion:** The itch to weigh in and see how others vote.

### Archetype 3: AI News + Implications (Plain Text)
* **Performance:** Solid performer (100–250 impressions). 
* **The Recipe:** State the news event in 1 line $\rightarrow$ extract 2–3 concrete implications for a solo founder or freelancer (using bullet points "•") $\rightarrow$ end with a debate question.
* **Key Emotion:** Staying ahead of the curve.

### Archetype 4: Story-Based Carousel (Visual PDF)
* **Performance:** Strong comment driver (highest average comments per post).
* **The Recipe:** 7 slides detailing a case study or real numbers $\rightarrow$ swipeable lessons $\rightarrow$ strong curiosity gap on Slide 1 (6–8 words max) $\rightarrow$ relative product screenshot embeds.
* **Key Emotion:** Curiosity and value preservation (saving the deck).

### Archetype 5: Data Visual + Hook (Visual PNG)
* **Performance:** Moderate impressions (50–100 impressions) but builds strong brand identity.
* **The Recipe:** Clean, cream-colored minimalist chart (#F5EFE8 bg, #E63946 accent) $\rightarrow$ paired with an interpretive caption explaining what the number means.

* **The Contrarian Hook:** *"Every founder wants to be CEO until they actually are."*

* **The Bold Transformation:** *"How we went from 0 to 40% conversion in 90 days."*

---

## ❌ 4. Anti-Patterns (What Is Underperforming)

Avoid these patterns to prevent low-performing posts (<40 impressions):
* **Plain News Relay:** Simply saying *"Company X launched Y"* with no analysis of how it impacts the reader's job, income, or career.
* **Double CTA Closes:** Asking readers to follow, repost, *and* sign up for a newsletter in the same post. Stick to exactly one call to action.
* **Polished Corporate Speak:** Using words like *"delve," "leverage," "vibrant," "game-changer," "foster,"* or *"synergy."*
* **Long Paragraphs:** Walls of text with no spacing. Use double line breaks and keep paragraphs under two sentences.
